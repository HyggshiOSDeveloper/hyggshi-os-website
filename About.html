Our Mission

Hyggshi OS was created with the goal of building a completely open simulated operating system on the Roblox platform. We believe that everyone should have the right to access, learn, and contribute to technology projects.

Our Vision

To become the largest open-source OS platform on Roblox, where young developers can learn, experiment, and create amazing user experiences.
