Today, Hyggshi OS's Hyggshi DMCA Report System Team research feature was deployed locally.

# About the Hyggshi DMCA Report System Team

The Hyggshi DMCA Report System Team is the official team responsible for developing, maintaining, and operating the Hyggshi DMCA Report System — a web-based dashboard built on Cloudflare Workers that allows users to submit and track DMCA (Digital Millennium Copyright Act) violation reports.

The system is part of the broader Hyggshi OS project center and was created to streamline the process of reporting copyright infringement, providing a transparent and organized way to manage intellectual property complaints within the Hyggshi OS ecosystem.
