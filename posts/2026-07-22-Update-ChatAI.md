Today we're rolling out a major update to Chat AI in Hyggshi OS Web Edition, bringing built-in image generation, a redesigned Collections experience, multi-model support, and a smoother creative workflow.

## What's New

### AI Image Generation
Chat AI now supports image generation directly in the chat window. Just describe what you want, and the model will generate it inline — no need to switch apps or tools. Aspect ratio controls (like 16:9) are also available for more flexible output.

### Multi-Model Support
You can now switch between models right from the chat header, including **Gemini 2.5 Flash** for conversation and **GPT Image** for generation. Pick the right model for the task without leaving your chat.

### Collections
Generated images are automatically organized into **Collections**, so you never lose track of what you've created. Browse everything in a clean gallery view, with Quick filters include:
- **All**
- **Favorites**
- **Today**
- **This week**

![image](/Resources/posts/22-07-2026/ChatAI2.png)

### Image Viewer Improvements
Opening an image now gives you a full preview with quick actions built right in:
- **Download** — save the image locally
- **Edit** — tweak the generated result
- **Share** — send it out directly
- **Delete** — clean up your collection
- **Favorite** — pin the images you love most

Navigation arrows let you flip through multiple generations from the same prompt without closing the viewer.

### Smarter Sidebar
The Chat AI sidebar has been reorganized for faster access:
- **New Chat** / **Search Chats**
- **Community** and **Coder** shortcuts
- **Projects** — open files and build something new
- **Collections** — pinned right above your chat history
- **All chats**, grouped by date (starting with **Today**)

![image](/Resources/posts/22-07-2026/ChatAI3.png)

## Why This Matters

This update turns Chat AI from a simple chat window into a lightweight creative workspace — generate, organize, favorite, and manage your AI content without ever leaving Hyggshi OS Web Edition.

More improvements are on the way. As always, feedback from the community shapes what we build next.
