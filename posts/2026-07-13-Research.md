# What happened to Hyggshi OS Web Edition?

According to user statistics, the number of website visitors reached 500 (this number is for reference only), which shows organic growth even as attention shifted to Zashi.

### The reason lies in the expansion of Zashi.

Zashi is a built-in messaging platform developed by the Zashi Foundation. The Foundation has signed an agreement licensing it to Hyggshi OS Research Technology (HORT) for further research into new features — typically free of charge.
